Privacy

This is a 100% client-side app. Your images never leave your device.

What we process

  • We process your image locally in your browser to generate the transformed result.
  • We do not transmit your image to our servers.

What we store

  • We store only your language preference in your browser (localStorage).
  • You can clear it anytime in your browser settings.

Downloads

  • When you click Download, the file is generated locally and saved to your device.

Third-party services

  • We use Google Analytics to understand usage and improve the product.
  • Google may set cookies or process usage data. Google Privacy Policy
